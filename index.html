Founded by Rohan R Shirke, Roshwealth is a modern wealth management firm built on trust, knowledge, and long-term financial guidance. Backed by over 10 years of experience in the Indian securities market and a strong foundation in finance and taxation as a Chartered Accountant, the firm helps individuals, families, and corporates navigate investments with confidence and clarity.

What began with managing family portfolios has grown into a practice serving 100+ clients and overseeing more than ₹12+ Crore in assets under management. Roshwealth believes wealth creation is built through disciplined planning, informed decision-making, and staying prepared across market and economic cycles.

By combining financial expertise, practical market experience, and modern data-driven insight, Roshwealth creates strategies aligned with each client’s goals, risk appetite, and future aspirations. The mission is simple: simplify investing, build lasting relationships, and help clients achieve financial confidence in a changing world.
